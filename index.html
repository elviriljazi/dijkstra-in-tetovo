Dijkstra in Tetovo!

Welcome to our website showcasing the results of our research project on optimizing order distribution in Tetovo using Dijkstra’s Algorithm! As team dedicated to exploring innovative solutions, we embarked on a journey to enhance the efficiency of order delivery in Tetovo. Through our research, we have utilized OpenStreetMap and the Leaflet library to implement Dijkstra’s Algorithm, a powerful tool for finding the shortest paths in a network. Our project aims to contribute valuable insights to the field of logistics and transportation planning. By applying Dijkstra’s Algorithm to the unique urban landscape of Tetovo, we have uncovered strategies to improve order distribution processes, reduce delivery times, and minimize resource wastage. Through this website, we share our findings and methodologies, providing a glimpse into the potential of computational algorithms in optimizing real-world scenarios. Whether you are a fellow student, a researcher, or an enthusiast in the field, we invite you to explore our project and join us in the pursuit of knowledge and innovation. Thank you for visiting, and we hope our research inspires further exploration and advancements in the field of logistics optimization.

Authors: Miranda Xhaferi, Elvir Iljazi
